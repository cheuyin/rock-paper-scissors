COMPONENTS OF THE PROGRAM 
- Title ("Rock, Papers, Scissors")
- Scoreboard for a single game (e.g 3-2)
- Message stating outcome of current round (e.g. "You win the round. Rock
        beats scissors")
- 3 buttons for the player to choose from: "Rock", "Paper", or "Scissors"
- A "Play Again" button that appears when a game is over

Player presses a button to choose either Rock, Paper, or Scissors

Randomly generate a computer choice

Record the outcome of the round and leave a message of it as well

However wins 5 rounds wins the game

A third button appears, allowing the player to start a new game
